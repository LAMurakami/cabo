Log

  • Published Page location: http://cabo.lam1.us/Weeks/2023/Winter.html
  • Sunday, October 1, 2023 Created this page
  • On Tuesday, September 26, 2023 I booked a 10 night stay at the Solmar Beach Club, Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico
    • I had to pay a $214.02 Deposit with $1,926.21 due at the Hotel for a $2,140.23 total.
    • I booked the return on Alaska Airlines for 30,000 miles and $92.14 and the trip to Cabo on Alaska Airlines for $390.14.
    • The total for the room and airline itinerary was $2,624 total plus the 30,000 miles.
    • I had been trying to book these dates for another trip to Las Vegas to use another MGM deal but for over two weeks the MGM site was down due the an attack on their computer systems.
    • Yesterday I had spent over an hour and a half on the phone and only got a quote of over $1,600 for the Bellagio and a quote of $656.47 for the Excaliber. I was being transferred to a different person to inquire for each resort. I gave up after an hour and a half because it was interfering with pool time and I wanted to take advantage of the two for one Happy Hour prices.
    • Today when I checked the MGM sites were still down and I decided another trip to Cabo would be at least as good as a Las Vegas trip and remembered that November is the only month that I had not visited Cabo. I cheked the prices at the Solmar Beach Club and decided just over $200.00 a day was good enough.
