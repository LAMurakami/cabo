Grand Solmar, Solmar Beach Club, Playa Grande resorts, & Hotel Mar de Cortez, Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico

  • Travel Days: Thursday February 23, 2017 & Tuesday April 11, 2017
    • I stay at the Hotel Mar de Cortez when I first arrive and then again the days before I depart Cabo San Lucas. I take a shuttle from and to the Airport to the Hotel Mar de Cortez for $17.00 USD each way.
    • I take a cab from the Hotel Mar de Cortez to the Grand Solmar and back from the Playa Grande resorts for $8 USD each way.
    • I walk from the Grand Solmar to the Solmar Beach Club and take the Solmar Shuttle from the Solmar Beach Club to the Playa Grande when switching resorts every two weeks.
    • I was given a Styrofoam cooler when at the Hotel Mar de Cortez initially which I gave to others on my second stay. It transferred beer and other groceries and was handled by cab drivers and bellboys between the resorts but not used otherwise.
  • Switch Resorts: Saturday February 25, 2017, Saturday March 11, 2017, Saturday March 25, 2017 & Saturday April 8, 2017
  • I learn my house was burgled and truck stolen
  • Vacation Days: Biggest chore: Where will I eat?
