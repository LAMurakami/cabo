In 2016 I moved the traded the Solmar weeks internally and got RCI trades for 2 of the Playa Grande weeks to end up with a Grand total of 6 weeks in a row at Cabo San Lucas. The final week was obtained by an RCI trade while I was already in Cabo. This week was a Spring Break week that I had started a search on to allow my niece to vacation in Cabo. My sister was with me in Cabo when the search finally came up with a result so that she would be able to return with her daughter in a few week while I stayed in Cabo for that interim. I had to return a day early to be able to lead the Democratic Caucus for my district in Alaska. My sister and niece also returned early leaving a Resort day unused.
