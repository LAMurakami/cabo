Cabo San Lucas Weeks


I am a Fixed Weeks Timeshare Owner in Cabo San Lucas, Baja California, SUR Mexico. This used to be the most popular way to own a timeshare but there are many other options these days. The major industry of Cabo San Lucas is tourism and one of the ways to make the most from a tourist is to sell them a timeshare. If you visit cabo you will be offered the opportunity to listen to a timeshare presentation.

Weeks at Cabo San Lucas

One of the best advantages of fixed weeks is that you can plan on that vacation on that date. I actually have fixed weeks with a particular room. I have to get there and if I have payed the annual maintenance fee, I check-in.

Fixed weeks start on a particular day of the week and are numbered starting with the first of the year.

Grand Solmar through 2018

I originally had a contract for 25 years on a 1 bedroom unit on the ground floor right next to the grand lobby and front pool bar of the original Solmar Hotel. This unit and the Grand lobby of the original Solmar Hotel and Solmar Beach Club were destroyed in 2010 and 2011 along with all of the original Solmar Hotel to make way for the new Grand Solmar. My Grand Solmar room was on the 8th floor of the 11 story building standing where the original Solmar Hotel 2 story wing by the rocks was. The room has a balcony facing the sunset and the beach in a building behind the main pool area with the entry doors facing the rocks.
