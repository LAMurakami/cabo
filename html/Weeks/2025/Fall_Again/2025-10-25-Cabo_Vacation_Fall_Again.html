Playa Grande resort, Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico

  • Vacation Days: Biggest chore: Where will I eat?
  • Travel Days: Tuesday, September 30, 2025 & Wednesday, October 22, 2025
  • Switch Resorts: Wednesday, October 1, 2025

Cabo Itinerary

I was upgraded to first class on one of the segments both on the way to and on the way back from Cabo and got to eat and have some drinks at no extra charge. I pay Alaska Airlines extra for main cabin seats so I can select a window seat and don't use the super saver fares that are not eligible for elite status upgrades. I don't pay for upgrades to premium or first class but like upgrades based on my Gold MVP status. This time I got Isle seats in the last row of First Class. I am O.K. with any seats in First Class but do like 1A when available. I was upgraded to premium for all the rest of the segments which means I can have drinks at no extra charge. For many itineraries I can select the upgrade to premium while booking the fare. Sometimes this includes the ability to select seat 6A which is the first row after first class which I like. Sometimes selecting 6A would require an extra charge but rows 10 or 11 can still be selected without an additional charge.

Log

  • Published Page location: http://cabo.lam1.us/Weeks/2025/Fall_Again
  • On Saturday, October 25, 2025 I found the extra charge that I had suspected when checking out of the resort.
    • When I was checking out I noticed that I was being charged a little over $20.00 too much and did a quick scan of the receipt and didn't see the issue. Today I saw that although all the charges were for a single drink which along with the tax and service charge is just under $20.00 I was charged for an additional drink on Monday the 13th. that included a $3.00 tip. I always tip in cash with pesos so this is not my charge. It looks like somebody else's charge got on my bill but it is now too late and I bought somebody a drink or two. I should remember to scan for TIPS in the description. I should have taken the time to get the bill right since I had an hour waiting in the lobby before the shuttle to the airport.
    • I ended up the only person in a small cab for the shuttle ride to the airport. The driver spoke English very well and we talked a bit on the way to the airport. He was driving a cab his brother owns and has lived in Cabo San Lucas his whole life or at least sine he went to school there.
