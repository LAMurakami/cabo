Playa Grande and Solmar resorts, Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico

  • Vacation Days: Biggest chore: Where will I eat?
  • Travel Days: Tuesday, August 19 & Wednesday, September 10, 2025
  • Switch Resorts: Wednesday, August 20

I have one night at the original Solmar Beach Club Resort before my three week stay at the Playa Grande Resort.

Cabo Itinerary

Alaska Airlines cancelled both the Wednesday, August 20 Seattle (SEA) to Cabo San Lucas/Los Cabos (SJD) and Wednesday, September 10 Cabo San Lucas/Los Cabos (SJD) to Seattle (SEA) direct flights I had originally used for my itinerary. I adjusted by adding a day at the Solmar Beach Club Resort so I could arrive Tuesday, August 19 and switched to First Class for the return itinerary.

Tuesday, August 19 Fairbanks, Alaska to Cabo San Lucas QDTRHJ

  • Depart Fairbanks (FAI) Tuesday, August 19, 2025 2:14 am Alaska 168 Seat 1A First Class

  • 2 hour 55 minute layover in Seattle (SEA)
  • Depart Seattle (SEA) Tuesday, August 19, 2025 7:55 am Alaska 1402 Seat 6A First Class waitlisted
    • Arrive Cabo San Lucas/Los Cabos (SJD) Tuesday, August 19, 2025 12:17 pm
    • Distance: 1,816 mi | Duration: 4 hours 22 minutes | Boeing 737-9 MAX

Wednesday, September 10, 2025 Cabo San Lucas to Fairbanks, Alaska IJFHDE

Log

  • Published Page location: http://cabo.lam1.us/Weeks/2025/Fall
  • On Saturday, August 16, 2025 I saw an upgraded status for the FAI to SEA segment and was able to select 1A.
  • On Wednesday, June 18, 2025 I added a day to the vacation.
    • I now arrive Tuesday, August 19 for a one night at the original Solmar Beach Club Resort before my three week stay at the Playa Grande Resort.
    • Alaska Airlines sent me notification of two SEA to SJD direct flight cancellations that affected my itineraries.
    • I adjusted by adding a day at the Old Solmar Beach Club Resort prior to the three weeks at Playa Grande.
    • The extra day cost $159.70 of which $39.93 was paid and $119.78 is due at the resort.
    • The Alaska Airlines schedule changes were at no additional cost but one added an extra stop because even on Tuesday there were no SEA-SJD direct flights.
    • New FAI-SEA-SJD Tuesday, August 19 itinerary
    • New Tuesday, August 19 Solmar Resort reservation confirmation
  • On Friday, June 13, 2025 I cancelled the UNXYQH reservation for the return trip and booked a new IJFHDE First Class itinerary.
    • Yesterday I had received an eMail about a schedule change that added an extra stop in LAX.
    • The suggested new schedule left me with only a 1 hour 41 minute layover to clear customs, go through TSA again, and change planes in Los Angeles, CA (LAX)
    • I was not comfortable with that and wanted a longer layover to clear customs and go through TSA again.
    • There were no great options so I chose a flight with a San Diego layover over 5 hours long.
    • I booked the new First Class itinerary for 35,000 miles which is 17,500 more than the main cabin itinerary I had booked.
  • On Tuesday, May 20, 2025 I confirmed a couple of scehdule changes.
    • The QDTRHJ change shortened the layover from 1 hour 55 minutes to 1 hour 10 minutes which is short but O.K. for on the way to Cabo when there is no customs.
    • The UNXYQH to FAI Sep 10 change adds six minutes to the layover changing flights only by a few minutes.
  • On Tuesday, March 25, 2025 I booked this Cabo vacation along with several others.
    • I booked Week 1, Week 2, and Week 3 for $1,195 each or $3,585 total for three weeks.
    • I booked 17,500 miles + $47.68 to Cabo and 17,500 miles + $102.71 back to Fairbanks as separate itineraries.
    • 35,000 miles and $3,735.39 total for airfare and the room. I will spend about $150 more for taxis and shuttles as part of the transportation.
