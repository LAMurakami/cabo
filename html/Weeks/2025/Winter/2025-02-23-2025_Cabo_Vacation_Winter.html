Playa Grande and Solmar Beach Club resorts, Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico

  • Vacation Days: Biggest chore: Where will I eat?
  • Switch room and Resort: Sunday, December 14, 2025
  • Travel Days: Sunday, December 7, 2025 & Wednesday, December 17, 2025

I am using an accelerated week 9 at the Playa Grande Resort and booked three extra days at the Solmar Beach Club Resort.

Cabo Itinerary

Sunday, December 7, 2025 Fairbanks, Alaska to Cabo San Lucas

  • Depart Fairbanks (FAI) Sunday, December 7, 2025 1:40 am Alaska 168 Seat 6A First Class waitlisted
    • Arrive Seattle (SEA) Sunday, December 7, 2025 6:12 am
    • Distance: 1,528 mi | Duration: 3 hours 32 minutes
  • 5 hour 38 minute layover in Seattle (SEA)
  • Depart Seattle (SEA) SSunday, December 7, 2025 11:50 am Alaska 714 Seat 6A First Class waitlisted
    • Arrive Cabo San Lucas/Los Cabos (SJD) Sunday, December 7, 2025 5:10 pm
    • Distance: 1,816 mi | Duration: 4 hours 20 minutes

Wednesday, December 17, 2025 Cabo San Lucas to Fairbanks, Alaska

  • Depart Cabo San Lucas/Los Cabos (SJD) Wednesday, December 17, 2025 5:15 pm Alaska 1401 Seat 6A First Class waitlisted

Log

  • On Sunday, February 23, 2025 I purchased the itinerary for $979.43 or $2,779.99 total for AirFare and room for 10 nights
    • I forgot and purchased a single round trip itinerary when I have been doing separate itineraries for each leg.
    • I was able to reserve seat 6A for all flights
  • On Tuesday, January 7, 2025 I booked this vacation.
    • Sunday, December 7, 2025 - Sunday, December 14, 2025 at the Playa Grande Resort.
      • This cost me $1,284.00 ($89 Acceleration fee and $1,195 Maintenance fee) = $183.43 / day
    • Sunday, December 14, 2025 - Wednesday, December 17, 2025 at the Solmar Beach Club Resort.
      • This will cost me $516.50 and I paid a 25% down of $129.12 and will owe an additional $387.38 upon check in = $172.00 / day
    • It appears to be too early to book the airfare on the Alaska Airlines site.
