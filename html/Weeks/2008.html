Arrive Saturday, February 22, 200 for Weeks 8 (starting 2/23/2009), and week 9 (staring 3/1/2008) at Solmar and then Saturday, Sunday & Monday nights at Mar De Cortez to return Tuesday, March 11, 2008 to Fairbanks, Alaska.

Weeks 8 & 9 were spent in H-1604. The days maked yellow were check-in / check-out days including the day I switched from Solmar to Mar De Cortez. The days maked lime are days with nothing to do but vacation.

Although I had always intended to vacation in Cabo for both my weeks 8 and 9, I actually got around to getting my flight real late. I used airline miles for the flight and the options were limited. The extra days made the return itinerary and time at the SJD airport as well as the trip to the airport a much more laid back experience than the Saturday arrival on the way there. Once in Seattle I was able too improve the itinerary but was charged a $75.00 fee. I was able to go to continue to Cabo on a single flight with a 45 minute stopover in San Francisco.
