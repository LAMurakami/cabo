My brother is planning his final Cabo blowout

2018 is the last year of the timeshare contract my brother and I bought together 25 years ago. The original timeshare room was destroyed in 2010 in the second phase of the destruction of the original Solmar Hotel to make way for the construction of the Grand Solmar.

My brother is planning for a family gathering on the final week of our original Cabo timeshare. He already has at least three units lined up for the week starting Sunday the 4th. of March and may end up with 5 or more. My brother is not looking at more fixed week fixed room timeshare deals in Cabo and this will be his final Cabo blowout.

Although the original two week Solmar timeshare contract is ending I had added three more Solmar weeks buying from other owners and not from timeshare salespeople. These were converted to units at Playa Grande with contracts until 2023 and 2024. This allows me to vacation in Cabo for five weeks in 2018 and continue to vacation in Cabo for years to come.

25 years ago the Solmar Hotel, Solmar Beach Club, and Terra Sol were the only things on the beach south of the Arch. There was also the Hotel Finisterra on the ridge above the beach and the grand total of all the rooms of all these was maybe 200 rooms. Today the Grand Solmar, Solmar Beach Club, Playa Grande, Finisterra, and the newest The Resort at Pedregal have over 1,000 rooms many of which are actually 2 bedroom and larger suites. The Pacific beach just south of the Arch at Cabo is no longer secluded and Cabo Peninsula has at least an order of magnitude more visitors than 25 years ago.
