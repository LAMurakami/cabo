The Ridge at Playa Grande resort, Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico

  • Vacation Days: Biggest chore: Where will I eat?
  • Travel Days: Sunday February 23, 2020, Wednesday March 18, 2020 & Thursday March 19, 2020 (RedEye)
  • Switch Resort / Room: Sunday March 8, 2020, Sunday March 15, 2020

The first two weeks are in my Weeks 8-9 Playa Grande Ridge Master Suite fixed Room D-3031. The third week (week 10) is the Internal Exchange for my week 27 1 Bedroom, 2 Bath Playa Grande Master Suite. It is possible I will have to change rooms Sunday March 8, 2020 but unless the Week 10 Owner for Room D-3031 shows up I will get to stay in the same room for all three weeks as I have for the last few years. With an Internal Exchange I am treated like an Owner rather than just a Guest.

Hotel Mar de Cortez, Cabo San Lucas, Baja California Sur, Mexico

I am staying an extra three days so as to not leave on Sunday which was not available for the minimum 17,500 miles award cost.

Breakfast is included with this booking.

Log

  • Published Page location: http://cabo.lam1.us/Weeks/2020
  • Saturday, November 9, 2019 @ 3:17:40 PM (Alaska Time) - Alaska Airlines changed every segment of my Cabo Trip.
    • I got notification via the app of a change of my itinerary but refused to confirm the change for more than a week. It was in Vegas or maybe on the way to Vegas that I noticed in the app that they had changed the itinerary. The part that disappointed me the most was that they had shifted me to a morning flight to leave Cabo. In addition they added a couple of hours to the layover in Seattle on the return trip.
    • Last Saturday I saw that there was plenty of seats on the flights 205 & 133 for the RedEye one stop return to Fairbanks that was more similar to my original itinerary so I called Alaska Airlines and asked if I could get it.
    • There was some mention of availability of mileage seats preventing it but I didn't have to fight hard saying there was not a problem when I booked the flight.
    • I was given the requested itinerary and even given a "premium" seat assignment as part of the change.
  • Friday, August 16, 2019 @ 12:01:21 PM (Alaska Time) - I got a second email confirmation that tentatively gives me room 115 at the Hotel Mar de Cortez with a mini-fridge as well as the breakfast each day. Room 115 is one of the ground floor rooms by the pool I had requested.
  • Wednesday, August 14, 2019 @ 2:44:29 PM (Alaska Time) I exchanged week 27 for week 10
    • I called 866-226-4169 (Resort Com, Inc) and payed $744.95 Maintenance Fees.
    • I then called 866-978-2578 (Solmar Reservations) and payed $189.00 for the Internal Exchange fee and got an eMail confirmation. This is a $933.95 ($133.42 / day) total for the 7 days of week 10.
    • I then made the 3 night Hotel Mar de Cortez reservation online for $209.64 ($68.88 / day) to complete the arrangements for the Cabo 2020 Vacation and got an eMail confirmation.
  • Tuesday, August 13, 2019 @ 5:14:06 PM (Alaska Time) - I booked the air travel for next year's Cabo trip for 35,000 miles + $99.57 and was able to get seat 10A and 10F and be wait listed for a first class upgrade.
    • The front leg is Sunday February 23, 2020 to match the start of my ridge weeks 8 and 9 and the return is Wednesday March 18, 2020
    • I will have to trade week 27 for week 10 at Playa Grande and book some extra days at the Mar De Cortez to complete the trip bookings.
  • Tuesday, May 1, 2018 @ 4:18:59 PM (Alaska Time) Created this page and linked it to cabo.lam1.us/Weeks
    • I haven't done anything for 2020 so if I pay the maintenance fee I can show up for The Ridge at Playa Grande D-3031 Master Suite (1 Bedroom, two bathrooms) Sunday Check-In weeks 8 & 9
