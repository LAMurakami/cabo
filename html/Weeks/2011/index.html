Saturday, October 2, 2010 @ 5:58:26 PM (Alaska Time) - Added the following to help friends and family that have weeks starting on Friday.

In 2011 the year starts on a Saturday and Friday and Saturday weeks are not aligned like most years. I am going to Cabo for both weeks and already have my airline tickets. Most years the Saturday weeks of any particular week number start the day after the Friday weeks of the same number. They start counting weeks after the first of the year so on years when the first day is a Saturday, Friday weeks of the same number start six days after the Saturday week. According to my calculations:

  • Saturday Week 8 Starts on 2/19/2011 but Friday Week 8 starts 2/25/2011.
  • Saturday Week 9 Starts on 2/26/2011 but Friday Week 9 starts 3/4/2011.
