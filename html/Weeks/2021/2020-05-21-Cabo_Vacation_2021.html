  • Tuesday, May 19, 2020 @ 10:33:06 PM (Alaska Time) - I will not book my 2021 Cabo Vacation until I know more about COVID-19.
    • I took my 2020 Cabo Vacation as planned but heard more and more about COVID-19 as the vacation went on.
    • By the end of the vacation Cruise Ship passengers were not being allowed off the ships in the Cabo San Lucas port.
    • Within 24 hours after I returned to Alaska an International Travel Advisory was issued by the U.S.A. that would have required me to return.
    • I have been practicing Shelter In Place since my return although I am allowing myself to walk to the Post Office and check my mail without violating my version of the restrictions.
